The PAX Token.

The native utility and governance token of the Paxeer Network, designed to power a decentralized, community-owned financial ecosystem.

Token Utility

PAX is an integral component of the network, providing key functionalities for participants.

Governance

PAX holders can vote on protocol upgrades, treasury allocations, and key parameter changes through the Paxeer DAO.

Staking & Security

Stake PAX to help secure the network and earn rewards from a portion of protocol revenue.

Ecosystem Incentives

PAX is used to incentivize liquidity provision, trading activity, and builder participation across the ecosystem.

Tokenomics & Distribution

A fairly launched token designed for long-term sustainability and decentralized ownership. Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 PAX.
