Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Paxeer Network.

What is Paxeer?

Paxeer is a high-performance L2 focused on professional trading infrastructure including an integrated DEX and settlement layer.

Which wallet can I use?

You can connect MetaMask or any EVM-compatible wallet. Smart contract wallets like Safe are also supported.

What fees does Paxeer charge?

Trades incur a flat 0.3% swap fee. Network gas fees are negligible thanks to our L2 architecture.

How do I earn staking rewards?

Delegate your PXE tokens in the Delegation Program to receive protocol fee rewards.
