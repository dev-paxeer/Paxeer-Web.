Paxeer Delegation Program

Stake, delegate, and secure the Paxeer Network while earning rewards.

Why Delegate?

Delegators play a critical role in decentralising governance and safeguarding the network.

Earn Yield

Share in protocol fees and inflationary rewards.

Strengthen Security

Your stake increases the cost of attacks.

Influence Governance

Delegated voting power shapes the future roadmap.

How to Delegate

  1. Connect your wallet to the Paxeer App.
  2. Navigate to the Staking tab and choose a validator.
  3. Enter the amount of PXE tokens you wish to delegate and confirm.
  4. Track rewards in real-time on your dashboard.
